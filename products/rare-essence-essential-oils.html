Rare Essence Essential Oils

Login to view price.
  • Description

Rare Essence sources the finest Essential Oils from around the world, working as closely with the plant farmers and distillers as possible. These oils are extensively tested by an essential oil chemist and are pure, natural & undiluted unless stated. 

There are Single Note Oils and Blends to choose from. 

Displays are available in 1, 2, 3 or 4 tiers and are a perfect way to display this beautiful line. Each Tier must be purchased with 12 different oils (6 units of each) and testers are included. These units are custom ordered to your choice so please allow 2-3 weeks for delivery. Price will depend on the oils purchased to fill the tier, ask for details.
